# Initial Launch

We're thrilled to announce the initial release of SaaS Starter Kit V2.

## Added

- Email/password and magic link authentication via Convex Auth
- Multi-tenant team management with role-based access control
- Subscription billing via Polar with free, pro, and enterprise tiers
- File storage and uploads with per-team quotas
- Marketing site with pricing, contact, and legal pages
