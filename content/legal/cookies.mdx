# Cookie Policy

_Last updated: February 2026_

## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to site owners.

## 2. How We Use Cookies

### Essential Cookies
These cookies are necessary for the Service to function and cannot be disabled.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `session` | Authentication session | Session |
| `theme` | User theme preference (light/dark/system) | 1 year |

### Analytics Cookies
These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the Service. They are only set when PostHog analytics is configured.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `ph_*` | PostHog analytics and feature flags | 1 year |

### Functional Cookies
These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `timezone` | User timezone preference | 1 year |

## 3. Third-Party Cookies

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

- **PostHog:** Product analytics and feature flags (optional, env-var gated)
- **Vercel:** Hosting and performance analytics
- **Sentry:** Error tracking (optional, env-var gated)

## 4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:
- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
- **Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies

Note that disabling essential cookies may prevent the Service from functioning properly.

## 5. Do Not Track

We respect the Do Not Track browser setting. When enabled, we disable non-essential analytics tracking.

## 6. Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
